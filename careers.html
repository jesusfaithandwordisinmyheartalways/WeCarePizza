Customer Support

Handling InquiresResponding to customer inquiries via phone, email, or live chat promptly and courteously. This includes answering questions about menu items, ingredients, pricing, and delivery options.

Taking Orders Assisting customers in placing orders accurately and efficiently, whether for delivery, takeout, or dine-in. This involves ensuring orders are processed correctly, including any special requests or modifications.

Coordinating DeliveriesProviding customers with information about promotions, specials, and upcoming events. This includes explaining any current deals or discounts available and helping customers make informed decisions about their orders.

Upselling and Cross-SellingKeeping detailed records of customer interactions, including orders, inquiries, complaints, and resolutions. This helps ensure continuity of service and provides valuable feedback for improving customer satisfaction.

Following UpFollowing up with customers after their orders to ensure satisfaction and address any further concerns or feedback they may have. This demonstrates a commitment to customer service and helps build long-term relationships with customers.

Resolving ComplaintsAddressing customer complaints and concerns in a professional and empathetic manner. This may involve investigating issues with orders, offering refunds or replacements, and taking steps to prevent similar issues in the future.

Adhering To PoliciesAdhering to company policies and procedures regarding customer service, including handling refunds, exchanges, and complaints according to established guidelines.

Training and DevelopmentParticipating in training programs and ongoing professional development to improve customer service skills and stay updated on menu changes, promotions, and company policies.

Food Prep Support

Ingredient PreparationPreparing pizza ingredients such as dough, sauces, cheeses, meats, and vegetables according to standard recipes and portion sizes. This may involve slicing, dicing, grating, and marinating ingredients as needed.

Dough PreparationMixing, kneading, and shaping pizza dough to ensure consistency and quality. This includes portioning dough into individual sizes and ensuring proper proofing or resting time before use.

Assembling PizzasAssembling pizzas according to customer orders or standard menu specifications. This involves spreading sauce, layering toppings, and sprinkling cheese evenly to create visually appealing and flavorful pizzas.

Cooking PizzasOperating pizza ovens or other cooking equipment to bake pizzas to perfection. This includes monitoring oven temperatures, rotating pizzas as needed for even cooking, and ensuring pizzas are cooked to the correct level of doneness.

Quality Control Inspecting ingredients for freshness and quality, including checking for signs of spoilage or contamination. Maintaining high standards of food safety and hygiene to prevent cross-contamination and ensure food safety protocols are followed at all times.

Stocking and Inventory ManagementMonitoring ingredient levels and replenishing supplies as needed to ensure smooth operations during busy periods. This includes rotating stock to minimize waste and ensuring proper storage of perishable ingredients.

Cleaning and Sanitizing Cleaning workstations, equipment, utensils, and surfaces regularly to maintain a clean and sanitary environment. This includes following established cleaning procedures and schedules to prevent foodborne illness and maintain compliance with health and safety regulations.

Assisting Kitchen TeamCollaborating with kitchen staff, including chefs, cooks, and other food prep workers, to coordinate food preparation activities and ensure timely completion of orders. Communicating effectively and working as part of a team to meet production goals and maintain efficiency.

Following Recipes and ProceduresFollowing standard recipes, portion sizes, and preparation procedures to ensure consistency and quality in every pizza. Adhering to recipe guidelines and portion control measures to minimize food waste and maximize profitability.

Adapting to Customer PreferencesAccommodating special requests or dietary restrictions from customers, such as customizing pizzas with specific toppings or ingredients. Ensuring accuracy and attention to detail to meet customer expectations and provide a positive dining experience.

Delivery Pizza Driver Member

Delivery of Orders Safely and promptly delivering pizza orders to customers' homes, offices, or other locations within the designated delivery area.

Navigating RoutesUsing GPS navigation or familiar routes to efficiently navigate to delivery locations while adhering to traffic laws and safety regulations.

Customer InteractionProviding excellent customer service by greeting customers courteously, confirming order details, and handling payments accurately and professionally.

Food SafetyEnsuring that pizza orders are transported safely and securely to maintain food quality and prevent spills or damage during transit.

Vehicle MaintenancePerforming routine vehicle checks, including checking tire pressure, fluid levels, and lights, to ensure the delivery vehicle is in good working condition.

Fueling UpKeeping the delivery vehicle fueled up and ready for use by monitoring fuel levels and refueling as needed.

Adherence to Policies Following company policies and procedures regarding delivery operations, including delivery times, payment processing, and customer interactions.

Cash HandlingHandling cash transactions responsibly, including collecting payment from customers, providing change accurately, and maintaining a secure cash float.

Maintaining CleanlinessKeeping the delivery vehicle clean and organized, both inside and out, to present a professional image and ensure a pleasant experience for customers.

Safety Precautions:Adhering to safety protocols while driving, including wearing seat belts, obeying speed limits, and avoiding distracted driving behaviors such as texting or using a phone while driving.

Problem ResolutionResolving any issues or complaints that arise during deliveries, such as incorrect orders or missing items, by communicating effectively with customers and coordinating with the pizzeria staff.

CommunicationCommunicating with pizzeria staff and management to provide updates on delivery status, report any issues encountered, and coordinate additional deliveries as needed.

Upselling and Promotion Promoting additional menu items or special promotions to customers during deliveries to increase sales and enhance the customer experience.

Flexibility Being flexible with scheduling and willing to work evenings, weekends, and holidays, as delivery demand may vary based on peak times and seasons.

Manager of Member Staff

Staff Management:You'll be responsible for hiring, training, scheduling, and supervising all team members, including cooks, food prep workers, delivery drivers, and customer service staff.

Operations OversightYou'll oversee all aspects of restaurant operations, ensuring that everything runs smoothly from opening to closing. This includes coordinating food preparation, managing inventory, and maintaining cleanliness and sanitation standards.

Customer Service Providing exceptional customer service is paramount. You'll need to ensure that all customers are greeted warmly, orders are accurate and timely, and any concerns or complaints are addressed promptly and professionally.

Quality ControlMaintaining high-quality standards for food and service is essential. You'll need to regularly inspect food preparation processes, ensure that all dishes meet company standards, and address any issues with consistency or quality.

Financial ManagementYou'll be responsible for managing the restaurant's budget, including tracking expenses, monitoring sales and profits, and optimizing costs to maximize profitability.

Marketing and PromotionImplementing marketing strategies to attract new customers and retain existing ones is crucial. This may involve coordinating promotions, advertising campaigns, and social media marketing efforts.

Training and DevelopmentProviding ongoing training and development opportunities for your team is vital for success. This includes coaching staff on customer service, food safety, and operational procedures to ensure everyone performs at their best.

Safety and ComplianceEnsuring compliance with all health and safety regulations is essential for protecting both customers and employees. You'll need to enforce proper food handling procedures, maintain a safe working environment, and adhere to all relevant laws and regulations.

Problem SolvingAs issues arise, you'll need to address them quickly and effectively. Whether it's a staffing issue, equipment malfunction, or customer complaint, you'll need to calmly and efficiently resolve problems to keep the restaurant running smoothly.

Team Building and MoraleFostering a positive work environment and building a strong team culture is essential for employee satisfaction and retention.You'll need to lead by example, motivate your team, and cultivate a sense of camaraderie and teamwork among staff members.
