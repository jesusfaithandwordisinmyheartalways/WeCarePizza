Individual Grilled Pesto Pizzas

For a scrumptious meal that’s ready in no time, these individual vegan pizzas really take the cake, or the pizza, as the case may be! The made-from-scratch crust takes minutes, then it’s a matter of slathering on pesto, a creamy white bean mash, and your choice of veggies. Note: The crust is made with white whole wheat flour, which is a variety of wheat that taste similar to all purpose flour but with all of the fiber of whole grain flour.

Ingredients

2 to 2½ cups white whole wheat flour

1½ teaspoons instant yeast

1 teaspoon pure cane sugar

½ teaspoon sea salt

1 15-oz. can no-salt-added cannellini beans, rinsed and drained (1½ cups)

1 tablespoon lemon juice

1 recipe Homemade Vegan Pesto

½ of a yellow bell pepper, cut into thin strips

¼ cup chopped red onion

1½ cups cherry tomatoes, halved

Crushed red pepper (optional)

PREP-TIME: 20 MINUTED/ READY IN: 30 MINUTES

Parsnip-Crust Veggie Flatbread

Not sure what to do with parsnips? Transform them into a savory flatbread crust that’s delightfully crispy but still sturdy enough to support your favorite toppings. While you’re welcome to make the crust and adorn it with whatever ingredients you have on hand, our version slathers on a generous serving of hummus and piles it high with the flavors of an antipasto platter. Roasted red peppers, tomatoes, red onions, and basil deliver Mediterranean flare while meaty jackfruit adds heft to each bite

Ingredients

¼ cup flaxseed meal

1 lb. parsnips, peeled and shredded (4 cups)

1 cup chickpea flour

¼ teaspoon sea salt

1 cup oil-free hummus

⅓ cup finely chopped roasted red pepper (from the jar)

1 14-oz. can green jackfruit, rinsed, drained, and cut into strips

1 roma tomato, sliced

⅓ cup slivered red onion

1 tablespoon red wine vinegar

3 tablespoons toasted pine nuts

1 tablespoon nutritional yeast

3 tablespoons sliced fresh basil

Apple Desert Pizza

Experience the flavors of a nourishing fruit parfait in a whole new way with this mouthwateringly good dessert pizza. Thinly sliced apples are arranged on the whole wheat crust and glazed with maple- and cinnamon-infused lemon juice. Clusters of crunchy granola provide a delicious textural contrast to the soft fruit, and a drizzle of vegan vanilla yogurt ties all the elements together. And while this recipe may be classified as a dessert, we think it would taste just as great as a grab-and-go breakfast

Ingredients

2 medium apples, cored, peeled if desired, and thinly sliced

5 tablespoons pure maple syrup

1 tablespoon whole wheat flour

1 tablespoon lemon juice

1¼ teaspoons ground cinnamon

1 cup purchased oil-free granola with no chocolate or nuts

1 cup plain vegan yogurt

⅛ tsp pure vanilla extract

Broccoli-Crust Veggie Pizza

Riced in a food processor, broccoli does a bang-up job in this deliciously green, gluten-free vegan pizza crust. Top with veggies such as avocado, asparagus, bell pepper, and tomato for a vibrant, healthy meal the whole family will approve of. As one reader says, “It tastes like heaven on a plate!”

Ingredients

4 cups broccoli florets

1½ cups chickpea flour

2 tablespoons almond flour

4 teaspoons Italian seasoning

2 teaspoons regular or sodium-free baking powder

4 cloves garlic, minced

½ teaspoon ground turmeric

½ teaspoon ground turmeric

¼ teaspoon crushed red pepper (optional)

1 cup unsweetened, unflavored plant milk, such as almond, soy, cashew, or rice

2 tablespoons lemon juice

2 cups 1-inch asparagus pieces

1 cup chopped red onion

1 cup chopped orange bell pepper

1 cup chopped tomato

1 medium avocado, halved, seeded, peeled, and chopped (optional)

10 to 12 fresh basil leaves, chopped

Thai Green Curry Pizza

Now you can enjoy your favorite Thai flavors in pizza format. The secret sauce? Thai green curry paste, lime juice, and fresh Thai chile, blended with oil-free hummus and frozen peas. This vegan pizza recipe maybe sound unusual, but trust us when we say the flavors are out-of-this-world delicious.

Ingredients

1 recipe Homemade Oil Free pizza Dough

1 teaspoon unsweetened, unflavored plant based milk

1/2 cup oil-free hummus

1/2 cup frozen peas, thawed

2 teaspoons Thai green curry paste

1 lime, halved

1 fresh Thai chile, sliced (optional)

2 cups chopped, steamed broccoli

1 cup chopped, steamed sweet potato

½ cup sliced red bell pepper

½ cup shredded carrot

¼ cup chopped fresh cilantro

2 scallions, sliced
